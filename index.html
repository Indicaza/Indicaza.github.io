About

"Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler". 

 - Albert Einstein - 

Software Developers work hard,  but because of that we're often a little boastful.  I definitely take pride in my work,  but I value clear communication.  I believe in building trust, and strong meaningful connections.

I love to make things,  learning to code started out as a means to an end but quickly turned into a passion.  It’s completely changed the way I perceive the world.  I'm grateful to be a part of this industry, and am eager to grow.
